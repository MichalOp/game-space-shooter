The Goal

Win. Don't die.

Shoot at your enemy with bullets and missiles, watch out for their weapons. Be careful - the forcefield, which was used to trap your enemy, seems to also be keeping you inside. Keep away from the edge - crash with the forcefield will destroy you ship!

  Rules

The game is played on a map 1920 units wide and 1080 units high.

You and your opponent each own one starship, which has 10 health points.

The ship has an infinite number of bullets, but only 8 missiles.

The bullet can be shot each other round, it detonates automatically at the closest point to the first enemy's unit that was in its damage radius. Hitting the edge of the board or ending it's lifetime (after 7 turns) also detonates the bullet. This radius is of size 100, the damage caused at the bullet's position is 10 and decreases linearly with the distance to it, reaching 0 at damage radius. The bullet is shot with the given velocities along each axis (in realtion to the ship), with the resultant velocity being clipped to be at most 100.

Missiles can be shot each round, as long as you have one. Their damage radius is 200, and the damage done is 20 in the center of explosion, decreasing in the same manner as bullet's. They have their own engines, so they can be moved around just as the spaceship. They are detonated with a command.

The ships start at symetrical positions, close to the center of the board.

Both ships and missiles move with the given acceleration along each axis, with the resultant acceleration being clipped to be at most 10.

  Game Input

Input for one game turn
Line 1: 1 integer units for the number of units on the board.
Next units lines: 2 integers unit_id, faction, being the unit's unique ID and faction (1 for the player, -1 for the opponent), 1 char type being Ship, Bullet or Missile, 5 floats health, position_x, position_y, velocity_x, velocity_y for the unit's health points left, its position on each axis and velocity on each axis.
Output for one game turn
(Exactly!) n lines, where n is the number of ships and missiles actively controlled by the player: Command for unit with unit_id in the form: unit_id | A x y | F x y , having one or more commands for unit_id separated by |.

Available commands:
  • For a ship:
    • A x y -- put acceleration x on x-axis, y on y-axis (with instant effect)
    • F x y -- fire bullet with velocity x on x-axis, y on y-axis
    • M x y -- fire a missile with velocity x on x-axis, y on y-axis (this missile will be receiving orders starting from the next turn)
    • W -- do nothing
  • For a missile:
    • A x y -- put acceleration x on x-axis, y on y-axis (with instant effect)
    • D -- detonate
    • W -- do nothing
Constraints
Response time for first turn ≤ 1000ms
Response time for one turn ≤ 100ms
